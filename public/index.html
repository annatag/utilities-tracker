House Maintenance, Utilities, Contacts & Finance Tracker

Tracks maintenance, utility bills, service contacts, mortgages, insurance, property taxes, sales tax, and tourist tax. Data is saved locally in this browser.

Items

    Do not store passwords here. Use your browser or password manager for credentials. This app stores provider links, account notes, bill totals, due dates, autopay, and paid/unpaid status only.
    Others: Use “+ Add bill” and choose Auto Insurance, Car Payment, Mobile Phones, Math Class, Soccer Club, or Other from the bill type dropdown.
    Tip: auto-backup downloads a JSON backup when data changes. Keep the newest backup file somewhere safe.
